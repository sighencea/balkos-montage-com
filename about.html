Company overview

From staffing to project execution

What began with industrial personnel support has grown into a full project-execution contractor. Today our core business is pipeline construction, industrial maintenance, shutdown services and project delivery — executed by people on our own payroll.

Personnel leasing remains available as a supporting service, but our identity is now defined by what we build and maintain inside our clients' facilities.

Mission

To execute industrial work safely and reliably inside our clients' facilities, with our own qualified specialists and a single point of accountability.

Vision

To be the contractor industrial operators trust first for maintenance, pipeline and turnaround work across the process industries.
