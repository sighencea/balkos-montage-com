Data Protection

How we handle personal data on this website, in accordance with the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

1. Data controller

The controller responsible for data processing on this website is BALKOS INDUSTRIE MONTAGE SRL, Str. Lac de Nuferi, nr. 18, Sc. A, Et. 3, Ap. 24, Chitila, Ilfov, România. Email: liviu.pop@balkos-montage.com · Phone: +40 744 431 889.

2. What data we process

When you contact us through the enquiry form we process the data you provide: name, company, email address, phone number, the service and industry selected, and your message. When you apply through the careers form we process your name, email, phone number, the position applied for, any uploaded CV or certificates, and your message. When you visit the site, our hosting provider may process technical data such as your IP address and browser information in server log files.

3. Purposes and legal basis

We process enquiry and application data solely to respond to your request and, where applicable, to take steps prior to entering into a contract (Art. 6(1)(b) GDPR). Technical log data is processed on the basis of our legitimate interest in operating a secure and stable website (Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR).

4. Retention

We retain your data only for as long as necessary for the stated purpose or as required by statutory retention obligations, after which it is deleted.

5. Disclosure to third parties

We do not sell your personal data. Data may be processed by service providers acting on our behalf (for example hosting or email providers) under a data-processing agreement, and is only disclosed to public authorities where we are legally required to do so.

6. Cookies and local storage

This website does not use tracking or advertising cookies. It stores a single value in your browser's local storage to remember your preferred language; this contains no personal data and is not shared.

7. Your rights

Under the GDPR you have the right to:

  • access the personal data we hold about you;
  • request correction of inaccurate data;
  • request erasure of your data;
  • request restriction of processing;
  • receive your data in a portable format;
  • object to processing based on legitimate interest.

8. Supervisory authority

You have the right to lodge a complaint with the Romanian National Supervisory Authority for Personal Data Processing (ANSPDCP), www.dataprotection.ro.

9. Contact

To exercise your rights or for any data-protection question, contact us at liviu.pop@balkos-montage.com.

Note: the contact and careers forms are currently provided for demonstration and are not yet connected to a live submission service. This notice will be finalised once form processing goes live and should be reviewed by legal counsel before launch.
